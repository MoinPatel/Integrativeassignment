Upcoming Events

Friday, November 15th, 7:30 – 10:30pm at the Centre for Social Innovation – Annex, 720 Bathurst St.

Have you been outside lately? The seasons they are a’changing, and you know what that means… It’s time to PARTY! Let’s come together to celebrate Not Far From The Tree’s biggest year ever, and more importantly the people who helped make it happen!

Come into the warm with good friends new and old for some Friday night drinks or simply show up to chill out. We have all kinds of party tricks up our sleeve. Prepare for awesome raffle prizes, tasty treats (think gourmet grilled-cheesers and apple crumble) and an array of libations to wash it all down and keep the fires burning.

$10 at the door / $5 for 2013 volunteers and registered tree owners *All proceeds go to support Not Far From The Tree’s picking program
