Don't miss your chance to win big this Friday

Not down with the hustle and bustle that comes along with the holiday season? With the crowded malls and folks just racing to complete their holiday shopping, the season can feel not-so-cheery. Well I have just the solution for you: this Saturday November 9th the Toronto Indie Arts Market presents to you their Early Holiday Market at the Gladstone Hotel!

There will be an abundance of merchandise, food and entertainment to help you get a head start on your holiday shopping. Whether you are looking for clothes, jewellery, or fun trinkets for an office Kris Kringle, there are vendors to meet all your needs.
