Volunteers are vital to the success of Not Far From The Tree’s programs. Each year, hundreds of amazing folks contribute their skills, energy and passion to help out in the field, at community events, and behind the scenes. We strive to create meaningful, action-oriented volunteer opportunities that will benefit the project, volunteers, and most of all, the local community.

General Volunteer Opportunities

If you’d like to volunteer, please fill out our volunteer registration form. We’ll keep you up to date on all the latest opportunities (including fruit picks!).
